Eren Yeager (エレン・イェーガー)

Eren was a former member of the Survey Corps. He was the main protagonist of Attack on Titan.

More info

In the year 847, Eren, Mikasa Ackerman (left), and Armin Arlert (right) joined the 104th Training Corps. The three graduated with Eren ranking 5th best, and they joined the Survey Corps following the battle of Trost District.
